About Post-Quantum Solutions

In a world where general Quantum Computing machines is a reality...

If you run an organization that must mitigate against such dangers, then take a look at the existing schemes that have been developed to combat the (super)-polynomial speedups that breaks current day one-way functions.

For your convenience, here is a summary of all the schemes you are able to choose between

Sub-area Capabilities Hardness Advantages Disadvantages
Lattice-based Encryption/Signatures Hardness of learning with errors and rounding Fully homomorphic, efficient, parallelization Large key sizes, key selection is hard
Hash-based Signatures Hardness of inverting one-way functions Efficient and well studied to be secure Signatures do not scale well, need to keep reliable state
